About Our Club

A branch of medicine focused on athletic injuries, prevention, diagnosis, treatment, performance, and rehabilitation.

Our Purpose

  • Educate members about the broad field of sports medicine
  • Encourage personal health, injury awareness, and wellness
  • Provide opportunities to expand knowledge through interactive learning

Typical Meeting

  • Informational slideshow presentation
  • Interactive activity or mini case study
  • Kahoot or Gimkit review game
  • Guest speaker or athlete/rehab discussion
  • Other fun hands-on activities
